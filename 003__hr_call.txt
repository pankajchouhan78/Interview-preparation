3. What is the difference between Django and Flask? When would you choose one over the other?

"Django is a full-fledged web framework that comes with many built-in features, such as an admin panel, authentication, and database management tools. It's great for larger, more complex applications where you need these features out-of-the-box. Flask, on the other hand, is a micro-framework that’s lightweight and more flexible. It's ideal for smaller projects or when you want to have complete control over the components you include in your app. I would choose Django for projects that require rapid development with a lot of built-in features, and Flask for projects that are smaller or require more customization."
